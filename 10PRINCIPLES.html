DIETER RAMS

10 Principles of Good Design

1

Good Design is Innovative

Design should be original, always coming up with new ideas and methods.

2

Good Design is Expressive Design should be expressive, whether of a brand or of an individual.
3
Good Design is Environmentally Friendly Design should be created sustainably and ethically.
4
Good Design Accomplishes a Goal Design should accomplish the goal of the designer or company it represents.
5
Good Design Sends a Message Good design sends a message from or about a brand, object, place or designer.
6
Good Design is Aesthetic Good design is aesthetically pleasing, connecting us to our ability to appreciate the world around us.
7
Good Design is Long Lasting Good design stays relevant and continues to draw people in over the years.
8
Good Design Draws a Reaction Good design first draws you in, and then evokes emotion.
9
Good Design Uses Space Wisely Design should use space wisely, ensuring that viewers are able understand and navigate easily.
10
Good Design is Adaptable Design should be able to adjust to new conditions and be modified.
